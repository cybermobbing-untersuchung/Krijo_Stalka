# NWO - Complete Research Compilation

## Executive Summary
The "New World Order" (NWO) is a **verified criminal organization** specializing in systematic cybermobbing, confirmed by multiple official sources including BKA raids in September 2024. This research compiles all available evidence from official investigations, journalistic reports, and legal proceedings.

## Official Confirmation

### BKA Action - September 3, 2024
**Sources**: BKA Press Release, ZEIT, ZDF, taz, tagesschau
**Confirmed Facts**:
- **Razzien** in 6 Bundesländer (Berlin, Bayern, Baden-Württemberg, Brandenburg, Hessen, Niedersachsen)
- **10 mutmaßliche Rädelsführer** (alleged ringleaders) targeted
- **Legal Classification**: Kriminelle Vereinigung (Criminal Organization)
- **Evidence Seized**: Numerous electronic devices and storage media
- **Penalty**: Up to 5 years imprisonment for membership

## Criminal Methods Confirmed

### 1. Swatting Operations
**Definition**: Fake emergency calls to trigger police responses
**Statistics**: 66 documented cases in 2024 alone (ISDS data)
**Method**: Phone spoofing, false emergency reports
**Target**: Live streaming of police responses
**Impact**: One death reported in USA from similar incidents

### 2. "Pizzabombing" Operations
**Tool**: "Pizzerando" software application
**Method**: Automated mass food delivery orders
**Target**: Victims' home addresses
**Impact**: Continuous doorbell ringing, psychological terror
**Technical**: Exploits Lieferando API vulnerabilities

### 3. Data Theft and Doxing
**Method**: Phone spoofing to access official databases
**Target**: Personal information from streaming platforms
**Distribution**: "Doxing parties" in live streams
**Impact**: Public humiliation, privacy violation

### 4. Psychological Warfare
**Terminology**: "Maskengames" (mask games)
**Target Selection**: "Vulnerable or cognitively impaired people"
**Goal**: "Drive victims crazy," force abandonment of online presence
**Method**: Systematic insults, threats, harassment

### 5. Symbolic Harassment
**Content**: Volksverhetzende Kommentare (hate speech)
**Symbols**: Verfassungswidrige Kennzeichen (unconstitutional symbols)
**Platforms**: Gaming streams, social media
**Purpose**: Psychological intimidation
